Gravity

MPM simulations can include gravity or any other body force (NairnMPM only). Use the following structure to specify a vector in the direction of gravity or the body force: 

<Gravity>
  <BodyXForce>0</BodyXForce>
  <BodyYForce>-9800</BodyYForce>
</Gravity>

The body forces are specific forces in units of mm/sec^2. For gravity, the magnitude of the vector is usually 9800 mm/sec^2.
