Gravity

MPM simulations can include gravity or any other body force (for more details see OSUPDocs wiki). Use the following structure to specify a vector in the direction of gravity or the body force: 

<Gravity>
  <BodyXForce>0</BodyXForce>
  <BodyYForce>-9800</BodyYForce>
  <BodyZForce>0</BodyZForce>
</Gravity>

The body forces are accelerations in units of mm/sec^2 (the force is mass times this acceleration). Any omitted forces are set to zero.
